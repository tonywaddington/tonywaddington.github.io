The Sage Apothecary

Branding for The Sage Apothecary – a 100% natural and hand crafted skincare company based in Wellington, New Zealand.

Branding created for The Sage Apothecary – a 100% natural and hand crafted skincare company based in Wellington, New Zealand. The Sage Apothecary was named for both the herbal nature of the product and the wisdom and tradition of using herbal properties in skin treatment. In creating a logo for The Sage Apothecary, I wanted to capture these messages and reflect them visually. To reflect the wisdom and tradition of the product range, I designed a monogram logo visually analogous to a traditional wax seal or stamp to suggest a ‘seal of approval’. To juxtapose the simplicity and authority of the logo, and reflect the creativity and vigour of this brand, I incorporated bursts of colour and playful typography throughout the branding. I also incorporated botanic illustrations and photography to reflect the brand’s natural ingredients. The branding has helped the company establish its own distinctive, home grown style, within the New Zealand skincare market.
