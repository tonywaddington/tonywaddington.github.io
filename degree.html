NTU Degree Show

Promotional materials for Nottingham Trent University BA (Hons) Graphic Design Degree Show.

Promotional material for Nottingham Trent University BA (Hons) Graphic Design Degree Show. The aim was to highlight the degree show as being a means of introducing new talent to the design industry. To demonstrate this visually, the concept involves the literal revealing of new talent. The tag line 'FIND WHAT YOU ARE LOOKING FOR' invites the viewer to look at the image, but the hidden message 'NEW TALENT' is only revealed when viewed through red acetate.
