Well done:

The symptoms of Addison's disease usually develop gradually. Rapid onset of these symptoms indicates acute adrenal crisis and requires urgent medical attention.

Many people recognise that a rounded face and truncal obesity are typical symptoms of Cushing's syndrome. Well done if you were able to recognise all the others. Notice too how these symptoms are also known side effects of high dose steroid medications.
