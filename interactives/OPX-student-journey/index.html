Learner becomes aware of a study opportunity.

Learner goes online to learn more about the courses on offer.

Contact made with learner via phone or email. Learning Advisor provides helpful advice and guidance, and supports the learnerâ€™s decision making, including learner preferences and cultural considerations.

Learner enrols online.

Easy to use technology supports learning any place, anytime. Materials can be downloaded, giving more flexibility to the learner.

Connection between learners creates learning community. Learners interact with peers.

Learners access academic support and mentoring online. Support systems identify learners who may need assistance with their learning experience.

Facilitators, subject matter experts and student mentors are in contact with learners to offer subject expertise, learning support, guidance and coaching.

The values of Ako are recognised and learners can be involved in teaching and learning.

Learners access different tasks and assessments, helping them to gauge their understanding of the learning materials, before they submit their assessments for marking.

Learners do assignments, exams and other types of assessment. These are marked. Feedback is given.

OP system reinforces success and prompts the learner on their next course or additional qualification.
