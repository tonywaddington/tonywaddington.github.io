OP VR Tour

In browser virtual reality walkthrough of the Open Polytechnic campus.

A virtual reality walkthrough tour of the Open Polytechnic campus. The tour includes hotspots that popup with information for relevant to that spot. The panoramic scenes are composed of some 20 plus photos taken on a Cannon 5D and stitched together. The finished build had to be delivered online, meaning a browser based solution was necessary. The output works both on desktop using the mouse to drag around the scene and also on mobile devices where you can move your phone to look around the scene. This enables it to work with VR headsets.

Work produced for and owned by The Open Polytechnic of New Zealand.
