Industry Week

A week of talks, workshops and lectures from some of the top names in the creative industries, at Confetti Institute of Creative Technologies.

Branding for Industry Week 2012 at Confetti Institute of Creative Technologies in Nottingham. Industry Week is a week of talks, workshops and lectures from some of the top names in the creative technology industry, aimed at giving the students the inside scoop on how to carve out a successful career in the creative industries. The event that year was the biggest to date for Confetti, so to match this larger scale the identity was designed to have more of a festival feel. The programmes were designed concertina, to be handed out on a lanyard as per a music festival. Similarly, the posters were designed to look like a traditional festival poster setting out the headline acts.
