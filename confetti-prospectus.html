Confetti prospectus

Prospectus design for Confetti Institute of Creative Technologies in Nottingham.

Read more

Prospectus design to promote the range of higher education courses on offer at Confetti Institute of Creative Technologies in Nottingham. The college specialises in Audio, Video and Gaming courses and has strong links within the industry. The design aimed to highlight the range and quality of the equipment the college has to offer, as well as to highlight the vibrant student experience on offer.

Work produced for and owned by Confetti Institute of Creative Technologies.
